    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>CSSS Officers Meeting<br>
January 4, 2010 </p>
<p>Present: Nader Beyzaei, Aleks Boulankov, Eason Hu, Mike Kenyan, Fergus Kung, Karen Lee, Kerry Li, Wendy Lin, Kevin Moore, Sonia Purewal, Alden Salgado, Jr&#xE9; Sarenac, Chung Voon, Ryan Wong</p>
<p>The Career Fair is next week. Google has confirmed attendance at the Career Fair. The total number of companies attending is around 25. We have 13 volunteers as well.</p>
<p>We expect $12000 in revenue and $7000 in profit from the Career Fair.</p>
<p>The other clubs (Cogs, Engineering) failed to recruit companies.&#xA0;Therefore it is going to be hard to justify sharing a portion of the proceeds to support those student clubs.</p>
<p>The advertising poster has been created. We need to print and put the posters up by Thursday at the latest. The VP Communications will return by Wednesday as well.</p>
<p>Encourage students to attend the department event &quot;How to prepare for technical career fair&quot; on January 6.</p>
<p>There is a secure ordering system for clothing already available.&#xA0;Our merchandise was ordered from the SRC last year. However, we will be looking into possible alternatives as well. It may be worthwhile adding laptop bags to our merchandise.</p>
<p>The VP Communications will hold a quick design contest for the logo in January. Order forms should thus be available in early February.</p>
<p>We may want to offer students the opportunity to order business cards. A website should perhaps be set up.</p>
<p>Lockers are now $10 per term due to student feedback and competition from the lockers in Forestry. Students who had already purchased a locker for the full year will be refunded $10 for fairness. The list of those students is available on the office computer.</p>
<p>We need to schedule the AGM. It needs to be well advertised to promote our club. We should couple it with a social event and also provide opportunities for student feedback in the form of a survey perhaps. Offer prizes for attendance and participation.</p>
<p>To promote inclusion of CS students, we need to consciously make an effort to greet students when they come into the office in a friendly but not creepy way. It may be worthwhile to start a game whereby CS students have the opportunity to meet new people and become familiar with the CSSS executives and officers such as a scavenger hunt with people as the &quot;objects&quot; to find or bingo boards.</p>
<p>The new furniture has arrived in the office. A big thank you to Fergus and Karen for help setting it up.</p>
<p>We need to create a poster advertising the food items we sell. Perhaps we can also get our advertising on the TVs in Reboot lounge.</p>
<p>We need to get to work on the weekly newsletter. The work on the yearbook also needs to begin.</p>
<p>The movie night is on Thursday. We will be playing UP and The Hangover. It is from 6pm to 10pm in Dempster</p>
<p>Due to a conflict in scheduling Research Night, the Industry Panel scheduled for January 21 has been cancelled.</p>
<p>The department is hosting an industry panel on January 28.</p>
<p>Laser Tag is on Sunday January 24 from 7&#x2013;9pm at Planet Lazer (the one located closest to Braid Skytrain Station. It is $5 for 2 games and 2 slices of pizza at Boston Pizza.</p>
<p>PUB Night on Wednesday, February 3 from 7&#x2013;8pm at the PIT.</p>
<p>Boat Cruise may be scheduled for March 27 or April 3. It will cost roughly $12000. Planning will begin shortly. We need to figure out our subsidy level.</p>
<p>Byte to Eat raised $830 and over 540 cans. We won the competition among the clubs!</p>
<p>Recruitment for tutors and tutees is ongoing.</p>
<p>There was an idea to host an open mike night. It was undecided whether this would be feasible. We are looking into it.</p>
