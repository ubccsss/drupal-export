<div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>Are you interested in being mentored by an industry or faculty mentor?  If yes, please come and find out more about the CS tri-mentoring program on September 20 (Tuesday) at 5:30 pm and join us for the orientation at 6 pm. Both sessions will be held in the Dempster Pavillion 310.</p>
<p>The info session will be held from 5:30 to 6 pm, and the orientation from 6 to 7:30 pm. If you have never been in the tri-mentoring program, please come at 5:30 pm to find out the program details. If you have participated in the program in the past, please come at 6 pm. Please note that attending the orientation is a requirement for joining the program. Juices and pop will be provided. Please bring a snack.</p>
<p>Please email Michele Ng at mng(at)cs.ubc.ca to sign up for the info session and the orientation.</p>
